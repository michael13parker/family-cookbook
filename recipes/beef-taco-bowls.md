# Beef Taco Bowls

All the taco flavor in a bowl — quick, and everyone tops their own.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 1 cup white rice (uncooked)
- 1 can black beans, drained
- 1 cup shredded cheddar
- 1 lime
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Cook rice per package directions.
2. Brown ground beef with onion and pepper over medium-high heat, ~8 min.
3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt, and the drained black beans; warm through.
4. Build bowls: rice, beef mixture, cheese, a squeeze of lime.

## Notes
Shares peppers, onion, lime, and spices with Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas — good to plan in the
same week to use ingredients fully. Add avocado or salsa if on hand.
