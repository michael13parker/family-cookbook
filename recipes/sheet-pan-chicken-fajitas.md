# Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas

Everything roasts on one pan — minimal cleanup, and everyone builds their own.

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb chicken breast, sliced into strips
- 3 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 yellow onion, sliced
- 8 flour tortillas
- 2 limes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tsp cumin
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
2. Toss chicken, peppers, and onion with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread in a single layer and roast 20–25 min, stirring once, until chicken is cooked through and edges char.
4. Warm tortillas. Squeeze lime over the pan.
5. Serve family-style so everyone builds their own.

## Notes
Great with rice or beans on the side. Leftovers make good burrito bowls.
